Model Visualiser

This is the current view into our ubiq.io Voice Verification Model® (VVM). It shows the distribution of vocal attributes across a continuum of perceived trustworthiness, classified by ubiq.io trainers, just like you. Our models are used by governments to maximise public budgets, expedite benefit application and eliminate fraud.

To cycle through the datasets, click the graph.

How to interpret

The dataset is plotted from left (least trustworthy) to right (most trustworthy). The Optimal Trustworthiness Zone® (OTZ) is shaded in grey and represents perceived vocal qualities most likely to be truthful. Amount of an attribute present, for example speaking volume, is shown on the y axis: the louder the voice, the higher the dot.
