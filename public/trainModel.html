Welcome to the training hub

You are helping to train our ubiq.io Voice Verification Model® (VVM).
Certain vocal characteristics may indicate the truthfulness of a statement. Your intuition about different vocal performances will help our AI model to continually learn about the intentions of human speech.

Instructions

  1. Press Play to listen to a voice from the database.
  2. Give the speaker a “trustworthy” rating from 0 (deceptive) to 9 (truthful).
  3. Use the Deciding Factors sliders to help our model to understand what makes a vocal trustworthy or not. E.g. amount of hesitation, how fast the speech.
  4. When you have entered your data, press the Save button.

To reload another voice, press your browser's Reload button.
Click the ML Visualiser tab to the left to see the dataset.
